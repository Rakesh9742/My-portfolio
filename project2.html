RESEARCH PAPER

1.In Metal Deposition and Additive Manufacturing: Bridging Theory and Practice at ASME

The utilization of memory foam as an inner lining material for vehicles through 3D printing technology represents a breakthrough in automotive interior design. Traditional vehicle interior materials have limitations in terms of customization, adaptability, and passenger comfort. Memory foam, with its unique viscoelastic properties, offers a solution to these challenges. By combining it with the precision of 3D printing, the potential for tailored customization reaches new heights.

2.Presented research paper in Aakaar, IIT Bombay 16th edition on Augmented and Virtual Reality for Architectural Visualization.

In the realm of architecture and engineering, the integration of virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) has revolutionized design. This technological advancement enables architects and engineers to craft immersive 3D models, offering profound insights into structural performance. Leveraging spatial mapping and gesture recognition, professionals can identify stress areas and address potential issues before physical construction, resulting in significant time and cost savings.
